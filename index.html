CHAI Lab Research Assistant

I am currently working as a research assistant at the Computer-Human Artificial Intelligence Lab. The overarching vision is to improve SER (Speech Emotion Recognition) systems by making them more aware of where emotion occurs. This can be helpful for downstream tasks like mental health monitoring, chatbots, and more to localize exact points where emotion is salient. Learn more about the CHAI Lab and what I'm working on!
